Flatbread with Creamy Tomato Sauce

Description

This delicious flatbread starts with a premade crust. Then you'll roast up some veggies and make a creamy tomato sauce with just a handful of ingredients. This all gets topped with shredded mozzarella and baked until the cheese gets ooey gooey and delicious!

Ingredients

  • 1 store-bought flatbread
  • 1 small zucchini
  • 1 small onion
  • 4 tsp italian seasoning
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 tomato
  • 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • 2 tbsp cream cheese
  • 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella
  • salt and pepped
  • your choice of oil

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees.
  2. Wash and dry the zucchini and tomato. Trim off the top and bottom of the zucchini, cut in half lengthwise, and slice into 1/4 inch thick half-moons. Cut off the top and bottom of the onion and slice in half through the root end. With one half of the onion, slice into half inch slices. With the other half, dice enough until you have about 2 or 3 tablespoons. Dice tomato and set aside.
  3. Toss zucchini and sliced onion on a baking sheet with a drizzle of oil and season with salt, pepper, and 2 tsp of the italian seasoning. Toast veggies until lightly browned, about 12-15 minutes.
  4. While veggies roast, heat a drizzle of oil at medium-high heat in a small sautee pan and add diced onion. Once onions are lightly browned, add in garlic, salt, pepper, and italian seasoning. Cook until fragrant, about 1 minute. Add in diced toamto and cook another few minutes, until tomato has softened. Add in balsalmic vinegar and cook until liquid has reduced. Turn heat off and stir in cream cheese. Taste sauce and reseason if necessary.
  5. Once zucchini and onion are done roasting, remove from oven.
  6. Place store-bought flatbread on a sheet pan and spread sauce evenly. Add roasted zucchini and onion, then top with mozzarella. Place flatbread in the oven for another 5-8 minutes or until cheese is melted and starting to brown.
  7. Top with parmesan or chili flakes if desired and enjoy!
